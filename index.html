Who are we?

The Bats Project is an organization that believes in bringing whimsical and memorable experiences to less fortunate children.

We believe in doing so by unleashing a colony of bats into the rooms of young people diagnosed with terminal illnesses.

Why Bats?

Flowers, balloons, and sweet treats are great for cheering up an unwell child for a day, but an unexpected visit from a family of fuzzy, flying friends is an experience that will never be forgotten. Most of our bats are not carrying diseases and will be the perfect surprise for a child who’s been met with a less desirable surprise.
